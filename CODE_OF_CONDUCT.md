# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

NiceCLI is maintained as a practical engineering project. Contributors are expected to:

- be respectful and direct
- discuss technical disagreements with evidence
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- keep reviews focused on code, behavior, and maintainability

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not acceptable in project spaces:

- insults, threats, or intimidation
- hateful or discriminatory language
- repeated bad-faith arguments or deliberate disruption
- publishing someone else's private information without permission

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, and other project communication channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer through a private channel when possible. Public escalation should focus on stopping harm, not continuing the conflict.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or lock content, and may restrict participation, when behavior violates this code of conduct.
